Welcome to your app dashboard! Both your Wholesale Tiers widget and Cart Abandonment Recovery widgets are installed. Follow the quick steps below to configure them directly in your theme.

Enable Widgets

Open your Theme Customizer and toggle ON the widgets in the App embeds panel to show them on your product and cart pages.

Open Customizer
2

Tag Customers

Open your customer list and add the tag wholesale to any accounts you want to grant bulk pricing access to.

Manage Customers
3

Discounts Setup

Create your automatic quantity discounts or standard discount codes (e.g. BULK5 and BULK10) in your admin.

Setup Discounts

💡 Need Help or Custom Automation?

If you'd like your abandoned carts to automatically create draft invoices and email them directly to your customers, enter your webhook URL (from Zapier or Make.com) into the settings panel of the Cart Auto-Recovery App Embed widget.
