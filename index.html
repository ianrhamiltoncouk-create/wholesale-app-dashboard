🔍 Detailed Guide: How to Edit Wholesale Tiers and Prices

  1. Click the green Open Editor button in Step 1.
  2. At the top-left of the Theme Customizer window, click the 4th icon (the one showing shape symbols: a square, circle, and triangle). This opens the App embeds sidebar.
  3. Switch the toggles to **ON** for both Wholesale Auto-Pricing and Cart Auto-Recovery.
  4. Click directly on the text **"Wholesale Auto-Pricing"**.
  5. A settings panel will slide open on the **right-hand side of your screen**! Change the quantities and discount percentages there, and click **Save** in the top right.
