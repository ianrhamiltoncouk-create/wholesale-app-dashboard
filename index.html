Step 1: Open Theme Customizer & Toggle App Embeds

Why do this? Activate the Widget Engine

Shopify Online Store 2.0 uses App Embeds to inject features securely without bloating or modifying your theme templates. Turning this toggle on allows our widgets to load automatically on your product and cart pages.

How to do it? 5-Second Setup

1. Click the green Open Customizer button in Step 1.
2. At the top-left of the Theme Customizer window, click the 4th icon (looks like shape patterns: square, circle, and triangle) to open the App embeds menu.
3. Switch the toggles to **ON** for both **Wholesale Auto-Pricing** and **Cart Auto-Recovery**.
4. Click **Save** in the top right.
